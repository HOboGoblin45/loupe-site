Your Dresser

A wardrobe you curate, not a cart you forget.

Every piece you save lands in your Dresser — sorted, searchable, and synced across devices. Build Looks, revisit favorites, and get a heads-up when something you saved drops in price.

  • —Save with a swipe. Anything you love, one gesture away.
  • —Build Looks. Style saved pieces into outfits.
  • —Price-drop alerts. We watch the items you love.
Style DNA

See your taste, distilled.

The more you swipe, the more Loupe understands you. Your Style DNA reveals your signature — your top brands, palette, silhouettes, and price sweet spot — in one shareable card.

  • —Your aesthetic, named and visualized.
  • —Top brands & colors at a glance.
  • —One tap to shop your whole vibe.
Make it yours

Style is better with friends.

Claim a @username, share your Dresser and Looks, and follow the people whose taste you love — all from one Loupe profile. You decide exactly what's public.

  • —Share your style. Show off your Dresser, Looks and Likes.
  • —Get discovered. Friends find you in the Loupe directory.
  • —Private by default. Nothing's shared until you choose.
Made to fit you

Dial your feed to your taste — and your size.

Set your budget, your sizes, your colors and silhouettes. Loupe shows you pieces that actually fit your life, and only the brands you want to see.

  • —Shop your size. Only see pieces available in it.
  • —Your budget. Set a range and stick to it.
  • —Your brands. Mute anything that isn't for you.
